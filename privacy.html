Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

Welcome to RozgarSathi. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us to ensure a smooth connection between owners and workers:

  • Personal Details: Name, phone number, and profile picture (if uploaded via long press).
  • Job Postings: Category of work, daily rates, number of workers needed, and work descriptions.
  • Location Data: We collect your live location (GPS coordinates) to show you relevant jobs and workers nearby.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the RozgarSathi experience:

  • To display your job postings to other relevant users.
  • To calculate distances between you and available jobs/workers.
  • To enable direct communication via calls or SMS between interested parties.
  • To show personalized advertisements based on your preferences.
  • To prevent fraud and maintain a secure environment for all users.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data in the following cases:

  • Public Visibility: Information in your job posts (Phone number, work category, location) is visible to other users so they can contact you.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for database management and AdMob for showing advertisements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. User Controls and Visibility

We believe in giving you full control over your data:

  • Hide Profile: You can enable "Hide My Profile" in the settings to stop showing your posts to others.
  • Edit/Delete: You can edit or permanently delete your job postings at any time from the "My Postings" section.
  • Account Deletion: You can logout or contact us if you wish to remove your account entirely from our records.

5. Advertising (Ads)

RozgarSathi uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. These ads help us keep the service free for you. AdMob may use device identifiers to show personalized ads. By using this app, you consent to the collection and use of this data by Google for advertising purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover a minor has provided us with data, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any updates.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:
